Presentation

I'm a Chemical Engineer, graduated on the University of Callao - Lima/Peru. Obtained M.Sc. degree from the University of Sao Paulo - Brazil. Received Dr. degree in Nuclear Technologist from Nuclear Energy Research Institute, Brazilian Nuclear Centre - Sao Paulo, Brazil in 1999.
My primary research activities centre around the subject of Radiation Vulcanized of Natural Rubber Latex (RVNRL) by Gamma Rays with particular emphasis on improve of the medical rubber goods.
The areas covered include Factorial Design, Response Surface Methodology (RSM), Optimization Chemical Process, Rheology and Characterization of Polymers, Economical Evaluation Chemical Process. I had published three papers.
